STEVE Harvey has been left "scared" of a Family Feud contestant after their intense face-off response on stage.

The gameshow's network shared the clip of the interaction on the Family Feud Instagram account.During one of the episode's contestant face-offs, Steve, 66, asked: "Tell me something that's known as a bloodsucker?" Laura, one of the two players competing for their teams immediately smashed the buzzer and growled out: "A man!" The comedian appeared to be disturbed by the answer as he stood motionless with a pleading, "help me" expression on his face. The studio audience could be heard laughing at Laura's response and the game show host's fearful reaction to it.
