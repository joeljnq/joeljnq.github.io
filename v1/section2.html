Hayden Panettiere paid tribute to her brother Jansen on Mondays “Good Morning America” in her first TV interview since his tragic death. Jansen died on Feb. 19 of an enlarged heart at age just 28.

While chatting about her new movie “Scream VI”, Hayden briefly mentioned the late actor. Michael Strahan offered his condolences during the interview, before Hayden thanked him and said: “Hes right here with me.” Hayden’s comments come after her family, including their mother Lesley Vogel and father Skip Panettiere, released a statement confirming Jansens cause of death to ABC News.
